## Food of insects

Finns have started to eat crickets! This is not a figure of speech or an eye-catching headline but downright truth.
Using insects for food is a new hot trend here in Finland. For some time, there have already been start-ups that offered
food made of insects.

A couple of days ago Fazer released their new product: bread with crickets. As the tag says, each loaf contains at least
70 crickets and all of them are of local origin. OMG, I already can imagine how them escape the fabric :-/
As you can see, Finnish customers like the product; I only could catch empty shelves.
